About



Hey, I'm Luís. I'm a Computational biologist at NYU focusing on the study of meiosis, the specialized cell division required for sexual reproduction. On my day job, I use Data Science to draw insight from deep sequencing genomics data. By night I let Data Science take me in other directions.

I started Data Simple as a platform to publish Data Science-related articles. It's still in its infancy, but I plan to post mostly data analyses and how-to articles.

I hope you find them interesting and I welcome your feedback, including constructive criticism and corrections.
